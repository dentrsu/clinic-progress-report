Verify Email Proof

Standalone — no DB access required

How to use

  1. Open the student's verification proof email.
  2. Quick: click the green Open in Verifier (pre-filled) button in the email — all fields fill automatically.
  3. Manual: copy the five values from the green Verification Proof block into Section A, then copy the clinical details from the email table into Section B.
  4. Click Verify Hash — the hash is recomputed and compared entirely from your inputs, with no database access.
Section A — From the Verification Proof Block (the green box at the bottom of the email)
Section B — From the Email Content Table (leave blank if not shown in the email)
